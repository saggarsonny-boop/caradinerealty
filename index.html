What We Do

Full-Service Development & Construction

From ground-up builds to complete overhauls, Caradine brings vision and expertise to every stage of the development process.

Renovation

Breathing new life into existing structures. We handle full gut renovations, historic restorations, and targeted upgrades that maximize a property's potential.

  • Residential & commercial renovations
  • Historic preservation & restoration
  • Interior & exterior upgrades
  • Systems modernization

Redevelopment

Turning underutilized or distressed properties into vibrant, productive assets. We specialize in repositioning properties that others overlook.

  • Adaptive reuse & repurposing
  • Mixed-use redevelopment
  • Vacant lot & blight remediation
  • Community-focused revitalization

New Construction

Ground-up projects built to last. Our construction arm delivers residential, commercial, and mixed-use developments from concept through certificate of occupancy.

  • Residential & multi-family builds
  • Commercial & mixed-use construction
  • Design-build delivery
  • General contracting

Project Management

End-to-end oversight so your project stays on schedule and on budget. We coordinate every trade, permit, and milestone from start to finish.

  • Entitlements & permitting
  • Contractor coordination
  • Budget & schedule management
  • Owner's representation

Real Estate Advisory

Strategic guidance for acquisitions and dispositions focused on development potential — helping you identify opportunities and navigate transactions with confidence.

  • Acquisition due diligence
  • Development feasibility analysis
  • Portfolio strategy
  • Disposition services

Community Development

We invest in people and places. Our community-focused projects prioritize affordable housing, neighborhood revitalization, and economic opportunity.

  • Affordable housing development
  • Neighborhood revitalization
  • Public-private partnerships
  • Community engagement

Who We Are

Builders Who Think Like Developers

Caradine Realty & Development was founded on a simple belief: that great buildings don't just happen — they're the result of disciplined thinking, skilled hands, and a genuine commitment to the communities we work in.

With decades of combined experience across renovation, redevelopment, and new construction, our team brings a builder's mindset to every project. We don't just execute plans — we help shape them.

Whether you're a property owner looking to transform a single asset, an investor pursuing a complex redevelopment, or a municipality seeking a development partner, Caradine has the experience, relationships, and drive to get it done.
